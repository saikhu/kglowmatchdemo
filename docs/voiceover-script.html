How to read this
Each slide has a target duration and an estimated word count tuned for a calm pace of about 150 words per minute. PAUSE markers are roughly half-second breaths. Lines in italic rose are tonal cues — read with a smile, slower, or for emphasis. The Aside column suggests on-screen motion, B-roll, or sound design to layer with the narration.

Producing notes

Pacing target is ~150 wpm. Total speech is roughly 326 words; with pauses and animation holds, the finished video should run between 2:10 and 2:20. If your voice talent runs faster (170+ wpm), pad the animation holds rather than cutting copy — the calm cadence is part of the brand.
